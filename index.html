about me

I am a first year master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I study Human-computer Interaction.

As a designer, I always try to remember one of the most important lessons I learned back when I taught English in Bangkok: that to be a great teacher, one must be a great listener.

I believe the same is true for designers. I strive to listen and learn from the individuals for whom I develop, just as I would my students.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio.
