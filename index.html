about me

I am a first year master's student at the University of Michigan School of Information, where I study Human-computer Interaction.I am actively seeking a UX design and/or research internship for the summer of 2017.

As a designer, I always try to remember one of the most important lessons I learned back when I taught English in Bangkok: that to be a great teacher, one must be a great listener. I believe the same is true for designers. I strive to listen and learn from the individuals for whom I develop, just as I would my students.

In my spare time, I love playing guitar, trumpet, and piano, taking long walks, and traveling. I'm originally from outside of New York City, but I've also called Bangkok and Hong Kong home. I am extremely passionate about noodles of any size, shape, or form.

Thanks for checking out my portfolio!
